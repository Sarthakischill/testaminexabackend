## 2. BRAND POSITIONING & COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

### Brand Identity
Excessive Peptides positions itself as the Equinox of the peptide space. Where competitors feel clinical and budget, Excessive should feel luxurious, confident, and science-forward. Think black steel, clean lines, precision engineering. Not happy-go-lucky, not pastel, not clinical-white-box.

> *"We want to be more like Equinox, kind of science-field. Theirs is more happy-go-lucky. Ours is closer to a more slick black and white type of feel, more premium. We charge more than them by a good amount."* — Phil K.

### Competitive Analysis: Amino Club (aminoclub.com)
Amino Club is the primary functional reference. Their site does almost everything right from a feature and content standpoint. The client wants to match or exceed their functionality while creating a distinctly different visual identity.

**What to Take from Amino Club**
- Account login / gated access before viewing products
- COA (Certificate of Analysis) section with batch testing links
- Fast shipping callouts and trust signals throughout
- Footer structure: returns policy, privacy policy, terms, disclaimers
- Research-only disclaimers and age verification
- Purity guarantee (99%+) messaging
- Community/support access (they use Discord; Excessive may do differently)

**Where to Diverge from Amino Club**
- Visual identity: Amino Club uses pastel, friendly colors. Excessive is dark, premium, slick.
- Pricing position: Excessive charges almost 2x. The site must feel worth that premium.
- Packaging: Excessive ships in insulated cold-pack boxes with mylar bags (not bubble wrap envelopes). This is a MAJOR differentiator to highlight.
- Manufacturing: Made in Germany. No other competitor markets this. Huge trust and quality signal.

## 4. SITE ARCHITECTURE & PAGE BREAKDOWN

### User Flow Overview
The site uses a gated model. Users MUST log in before seeing any products. This is different from most peptide competitors (including Amino Club) who show products on the homepage.

**Flow: Landing Page (public) > Login / Create Account > Terms of Use Agreement > Full Site Access (products, COAs, account, orders)**

### Page 1: Landing / Splash Page (Public)
This is the only page visible to non-authenticated users. It must accomplish three things: establish premium brand identity, communicate core value props, and drive login/account creation.

**Required Elements**
1. Hero Section: Full-screen dark background with brand name, tagline, and single CTA (Log In / Create Account). No product images visible.
2. Value Prop Strip: 3-4 key differentiators with icons (Made in Germany, Cold-Chain Shipping, 99%+ Purity, Third-Party Tested)
3. Tagline / Positioning Statement: Something conveying pharmaceutical-grade, premium research peptides. Not fluffy.
4. Login/Register CTA: Prominent button. Consider glass-morphism or elevated card treatment.
5. Research Disclaimer: Footer area with standard research-use-only language

> *DESIGNER NOTE: The landing page is the first impression for a brand that charges 2x the competition. It has to feel like walking into an Equinox lobby, not a GNC.*

### Page 2: Login / Registration
Clean modal or dedicated page. Account creation captures email and creates the login relationship used for commission tracking (each customer is tagged to a salesperson via login).

## 5. KEY SELLING POINTS TO HIGHLIGHT

These are the differentiators that justify the premium price point. They should be prominently featured throughout the site, not buried in copy. Each one deserves its own visual treatment.

### 1. Made in Germany
No other competitor in this space markets German manufacturing. This is a massive trust signal. Use it everywhere. Consider a dedicated icon or badge.

> *Suggested visual: German flag element subtly integrated into trust badges. Precision/engineering imagery. Manufacturing facility photos if available.*

### 2. Cold-Chain Insulated Shipping
Competitors ship in padded envelopes at room temperature. Excessive ships in insulated boxes with ice packs. This directly impacts product quality because peptides degrade in heat during transit. This is a huge differentiator and should get a dedicated storytelling section.

> *Suggested visual: Scroll-triggered animation or infographic showing the shipping process. Vial > mylar bag > cardboard insert > insulated box with ice pack > delivered. Compare side-by-side with competitor's bubble wrap envelope.*

### 3. Premium Packaging
Products come in professionally designed mylar bags with inner cardboard packaging. This is borrowed from the cannabis industry (Stiiizy connection). The unboxing experience is intentionally elevated above every competitor.

> *Suggested visual: Unboxing photo sequence or product photography showing the packaging layers. Hero-quality shots of the mylar bags and inner packaging.*

### 4. 99%+ Purity with Third-Party Testing
Industry standard claim, but back it up with accessible COAs. Every batch should have a clickable link to its Certificate of Analysis.

## 6. UX & FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

### Authentication / Gating
- Entire store is gated behind login. No product images, prices, or details visible without authentication.
- Login is used for commission tracking. Each user is tagged to the salesperson who referred them.
- Terms of Use acceptance must happen during registration (research purposes, legal compliance).
- Age verification checkbox at minimum.

### E-Commerce Integration
- The front-end is custom HTML/CSS/JS. Back-end order management will be handled via Squarespace.
- Payment processing is handled by the client team (experienced with gray-market payment solutions from cannabis).
- Discount codes must be supported (used for affiliate/commission tracking and promotions).
- Cart and checkout must feel native to the custom design, not a jarring Squarespace redirect.

### Responsive / Mobile
- Fully responsive. Mobile-first design given that traffic will likely come from social and referral channels.
- Maintain premium feel on mobile. No compromising the dark, clean aesthetic for mobile shortcuts.
- Touch-friendly product cards and checkout flow.

### Performance
Speed is critical. The client emphasized wanting the fastest possible build and launch. The site itself should also load fast. Optimize images, minimize JS bloat, lazy-load below-fold content.

## 8. CONTENT & COPY DIRECTION

### Tone of Voice
Confident, direct, technical, premium. Not clinical or boring, but not casual or playful either. Think: a world-class lab that also happens to have incredible branding. No fluff. No over-promising. Let the product quality speak.

| Do | Don't |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Pharmaceutical-grade purity, verified by independent labs." | "We make the best peptides ever! You'll love them!" |
| "Manufactured in Germany. Cold-chain shipped to your door." | "We care about quality and we ship fast!" |
| "Every batch tested. Every result published." | "Our peptides are totally safe and amazing!" |
| "Research-grade compounds for qualified investigators." | "Try our peptides today for great results!" |

### Legal / Compliance Copy
Every page needs a research-use-only disclaimer. Reference Amino Club's legal structure and adapt for Excessive. Key disclaimers needed:
- "All products are sold strictly for research, laboratory, and educational purposes only."
- "Not intended for human or veterinary use."
- "Statements have not been evaluated by the FDA."
- Age verification requirement
- Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, Returns & Refund Policy (reference Amino Club structure, adapt wording)
